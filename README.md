# House-Price-Prediction-Model
# We took housing market data of Ames,Iowa and created 4 regression models to predict housing data. Although our models performed very well (We had an RMSE that would put us in the top 60 on the keggle competition), I decided to take things a step further and use our data exploration and models to understand and explain all its business interpretations and benefits to stakeholders. What are buyers looking for at specific price points? What do renovators and builders need to focus on for the highest ROI? These questions plus many more were answered in our presentation.
